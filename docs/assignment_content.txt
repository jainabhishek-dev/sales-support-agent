AI Builder Take-Home
What this is (30-second read)
At Scaler, sales happens on a phone call. A BDA warms up a lead, calls them, tries to
understand their goals, answers their doubts, and nudges them to take an entrance test. Most
drop off ΓÇö before the call, during the call, or after. Each drop-off is a place where AI can
supercharge the BDA.
Your job: build an agent that supercharges two of those moments ΓÇö the pre-sales WhatsApp
nudge to the BDA (warming them up about the lead before the call, driving pickup and call
quality), and the post-call personalised PDF to the lead (building enough trust to get them to the
test). Both delivered on WhatsApp.
5 hours of work. We respect your time and we'll read it as a judgment signal if you go
significantly over.
Why we're hiring for this
Scaler is going AI-native end-to-end. Sales is where we're starting. If your prototype makes one
BDA meaningfully more effective at converting leads into enrolments, we roll it out and you own
the roadmap. This is a real slice of that roadmap, not a puzzle.
An invitation, up front
If you read this brief and have a genuinely better idea for how AI should supercharge a BDA at

How sales at Scaler works
1. Lead is created ΓÇö through a marketing campaign (MC), organic search, or re-activation
of an old lead that went cold2. Lead is assigned to a BDA ΓÇö a Business Development Associate on the sales team
3. Pre-call ΓÇö today, the BDA usually walks into the call with nothing more than the lead's
form data in the CRM. No context on who this specific person is, what's likely driving
them, what objections to expect, or how to open. The first 10 seconds of the call are
generic ("hi, this is X from Scaler") and leads hang up. Both pickup rates and call quality
suffer
4. The call ΓÇö the BDA pulls the lead's details from the CRM and calls them. They try to
talk, stretch the call, build connect, understand where the lead is today, sketch a
roadmap for where they could be, and answer their questions. Many calls die inside 60
seconds because connect doesn't happen. Others die because the lead asks technical
questions about program depth ΓÇö "is your AI curriculum updated?", "how deep on
system design?" ΓÇö that the BDA isn't technical enough to answer confidently
5. Post-call ΓÇö if the call went well, the BDA nudges the lead to take the entrance test.
Clearing the test is the gate to the second call and enrolment. Most leads who had a
good call still never take the test ΓÇö because nothing between the call and the test
builds enough trust to make it worth their time
6. Drop-offs happen at every step. The two places where AI gives the biggest lift are at
steps 3 and 5 ΓÇö prepping the BDA before the call so they open it better, and building
lead trust after it so they actually take the test
What you'll build
Two pieces. Both delivered over WhatsApp. The pre-sales nudge goes to the BDA (no approval
step ΓÇö it's internal). The post-call PDF goes to the lead, and every lead-facing send is routed
through the BDA for Approve / Edit / Skip ΓÇö nothing lead-facing fires automatically.
1. Pre-sales WhatsApp nudge to the BDA ΓÇö ~1 hour
Sent to the BDA on their own WhatsApp, ahead of the call, so they walk in prepared and the
first 30 seconds of the call don't sound generic. Better openings drive both pickup rate and call
quality up significantly.
Good looks like:
- Short and scannable ΓÇö the BDA is reading this on their phone 2 minutes before dialling,
not studying it
- Specific to this lead: who they are in plain English, their likely persona and why, two or
three angles that'll resonate (each tied to something real about them), two or three
objections to expect with a one-line handle, a suggested opening hook
- Honest about what's inferred vs. fact vs. missing
- Written like a teammate's message, not a corporate memoNo approval gate here ΓÇö this is the agent briefing the BDA, not speaking to a lead.
2. Post-call personalised PDF, delivered on WhatsApp ΓÇö ~3 hours
After the call, generate a 2ΓÇô3 page PDF that:
- Addresses each of the lead's open questions specifically, with evidence (curriculum
detail, alumni outcomes from similar learners, concrete ROI reasoning for their current
package)
- Frames Scaler's strength through the lens of this lead's goals, not generic marketing
- Is visibly different lead-to-lead ΓÇö Rohan's PDF should not look or read like Karthik's
- Is branded, readable, and something a lead would actually open on their phone
- Gets delivered to the lead's WhatsApp with a short personalised covering message
Your agent must accept two input modes:
- Structured: a lead profile + a call transcript (text)
- Audio: a lead profile + a call recording (audio file)
In the audio path, you transcribe, extract the lead's open questions, and generate the PDF from
the extracted content. Whisper, AssemblyAI, Deepgram ΓÇö your call.
The deployed app must support both input modes. We will upload our own audio/transcript +
profile during demo review and see what your agent produces. Don't hardcode for the three
standard inputs below.
